# 🧺 Iconic Indian Detergent Brand Redesign

## Overview
The website has been completely reimagined to compete with top-tier Indian detergent brands like **Surf Excel**, **Ariel**, and **Rin**. The new design focuses on **Trust**, **Freshness**, and **Cleaning Power** while maintaining an approachable, family-friendly vibe.
(This redesign overwrites the previous version.)

## ✨ Key Design Features

### 1. **Visual Identity**
- **Palette:** Ocean Blue (Power), Fresh Green (Safety), Energy Orange (Action).
- **Motifs:** Bubbles, Splashes, Sparkles ✨, "White" usage for cleanliness.
- **Animations:** Floating bubbles in the Hero background, "Splash" effects on product packs.

### 2. **Hero Section**
- **Headline:** *"Tough on Stains, Gentle on You!"* - A classic, reassuring value proposition.
- **Trust Badges:** "100% Safe", "Trusted by 10 Lakh+ Homes".
- **Visuals:** Simulated 3D product pack with "New Formula" badge and splash effects.

### 3. **Product Range Section**
- Clearly segmented products:
  - **Active Powder:** Blue/Orange theme.
  - **Gentle Liquid:** Green/Pink theme.
  - **Floor Shine:** Purple/Blue theme.
  - **Stain Bar:** Orange/Red theme.
- **Pricing:** Clear Rupee symbol formatting (e.g., ₹85/kg).

### 4. **"Indian Trust" Section**
- Testimonials representing diverse regions (Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad, etc.).
- "Made in India" 🇮🇳 badging prominently displayed.
- Relatable quotes focusing on affordability and effectiveness.

### 5. **How It Works**
- Simple 3-step process: **Penetrates → Lifts → Protects**.
- Visualized with large, clear icons and connecting graphics.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
- **Tailwind CSS:** Used for all styling, ensuring responsiveness.
- **GSAP:** Powered complex animations (Entrance fades, scroll triggers).
- **CSS Shapes:** Created "Product Packs" and "Bubbles" using pure CSS (no external images required).

# 🇮🇳 Iconic Indian Detergent Aesthetics Update

## Overview
The website design has been refined to closely match the visual identity of iconic Indian detergent brands like **Ariel**, **Surf Excel**, and **Rin**.

## 🎨 Design Updates

### 1. **Color Palette Refinement**
- **Royal Blue & Ocean Blue:** The primary dominant colors, representing deep cleaning and freshness.
- **Fresh Green:** Used for "Nature", "Eco-friendly", and "Freshness" cues.
- **Energy Orange/Red:** High-contrast accents for "Stain Removal", "Power", and Call-to-Actions.
- **Sparkling White:** The canvas background to emphasize "Whitest White" results.

### 2. **Hero Section Transformation**
- **Background:** Switched to a **Radial Gradient (White → Blue)**. This mimics the "Shiny/Clean Surface" look found in detergent commercials.
- **Sparkle Motifs:** Added animated ✨ sparkles using custom CSS shapes to visually represent "Shine" and "Cleanliness".
- **Typography:**
    - Huge, Impactful Hindi/English blend: **"DAGHO KA DUSHMAN!"**.
    - "No. 1 Stain Removal Technology" badge in Green.
    - High-contrast text shadows for maximum readability.

### 3. **Navbar Modernization**
- **Style:** Clean **White Background** with a crisp **Blue Bottom Border**.
- **Logo:** Updated to a "Swirl" icon representing water/cleaning action.
- **Interactions:** Green underscore animations on hover.

### 4. **CSS & Animations**
- **`index.css`**: Added `.clip-star` utility for creating geometric sparkle shapes without images.
- **Scrollbar:** Customized to a Blue-Green gradient.
- **Global Typography:** Switched headings to **Montserrat (ExtraBold)** for that commercial, authoritative look.

## 🚀 Key Visual Cues
- **Swirls & Blobs:** Representing liquid movement and cleaning action.
- **Pulse & Ping:** Animations on badges to draw attention to "New" and "Best Seller" tags.
- **White Space:** Increased to make the colorful elements pop, just like on product packaging.

---

**Status:** The application is running with the new design applied.
